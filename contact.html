Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to prepare for IELTS?

The preparation time varies depending on your current English level and target score. Typically, our students complete preparation in 8-12 weeks with consistent practice.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes! We offer both online and in-person classes. Online classes are conducted via Zoom with live interaction, just like in-person sessions.

What is the class size for group sessions?

We keep our group classes small with a maximum of 6 students to ensure everyone gets adequate attention and practice time.

Can I get a free trial class?

Yes, we offer a free consultation session where you can discuss your goals, take an assessment, and experience our teaching style before enrolling.

What study materials are provided?

All enrolled students receive comprehensive study materials including practice tests, sample answers, vocabulary lists, and access to online resources.
